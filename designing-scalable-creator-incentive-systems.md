# Designing Scalable Creator Incentive Systems

## Background

As creator ecosystems grow, incentive systems become increasingly difficult to scale.

Different creator segments require different motivations while operational costs must remain manageable.

---

## Problem

How can incentive systems encourage participation while remaining sustainable?

---

## Constraints

- Limited budget
- Multiple creator segments
- Different creator motivations
- Operational complexity

---

## My Role

I designed and operated creator incentive programs, collaborating with operations, regional teams, and data stakeholders.

---

## Approach

Instead of applying a single incentive model, I focused on segmentation and scalable program design.

Key principles included:

- Tier-based incentives
- Clear qualification criteria
- Operational simplicity
- Long-term sustainability
